# Freight-Cost-Optimisation
Freight Cost Optimisation Dashboard

A consulting-style freight cost analysis across global supply chain shipment modes and destinations.

Objective: Identify freight cost inefficiencies and recommend optimisation strategies across shipment modes and routes.

Tools Used: Microsoft Excel, Pivot Tables, Dashboard, KPI Analysis

Dataset: 10,324 shipment records across 40+ countries and 4 shipment modes

Key Findings:

Air freight consumes 64% of total $67M budget despite costing $5.28 per KG - most expensive mode
Truck is most cost efficient at $1.56 per KG - 3.4x cheaper than Air
Cameroon is most expensive destination at $32,006 average freight cost
Ocean shipping severely underutilised at only 5% of total spend

Recommendations:

Shift 20% of Air volume to Ocean or Truck - save $3-4M annually
Increase Ocean freight for non-urgent heavy shipments
Negotiate dedicated carrier contracts for high cost African routes

Expected Impact: 15-20% reduction in total freight spend
